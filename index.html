Joseph Hughes

Joseph Hughes is an Amsterdam-based post-disciplinary artist working across sculpture, objects, installation, film and publishing. Rooted in bricolage and assemblage, his practice draws on everyday products, found materials, functional forms and cultural symbols.

He recombines familiar things until their logic begins to unravel, creating works that sit between usefulness and uselessness, sincerity and absurdity. With his tongue firmly in cheek, Hughes uses material contradiction, visual puns and disrupted function to question authenticity, value, authorship and the authority embedded in design.
